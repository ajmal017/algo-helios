FAQ

  • What are index funds ?

    An index fund is a mutual fund or exchange-traded fund designed to follow certain preset rules so that the fund can track a specified index.

  • Who should invest ?

    Any individual (Resident Indian or NRI) can transact with Algonauts. This is an ideal product for a Risk-averse investor.

  • What is the minimum investment ?

    Investors can start investing in this fund with a minimum investment amount of Rs. 1 lakh lump-sum or SIP of Rs. 10,000

  • How is Smart Index Fund different from regular index funds ?

    SIF is smarter than usual index funds as it invests in selective stocks of the index decided by the algorithm with the same weights, thereby ensuring that the underperforming stocks are avoided and hold onto the outperforming stocks.

  • How Smart Index Fund manages to outperform the Regular Index Fund ?

    It is achieved by an inhouse proprietary algorithm that scans the stocks 24x7 to throw out the analysis of the stocks, which in turn filters out the weak stocks thus making sure that the SIF always outperforms the regular index funds.
