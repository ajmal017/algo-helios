What We Do ?

  • Price Analysis

Price Analysis

  • Algorithm generates target price & stop loss combination by analyzing the historical chart
  • This combination maximizes probability of making profits over given time horizon for a given algorithm
  • Signal-to-signal trading is completely avoided by having a target price and stop loss
  • Being in the market full-time is the surest way for an algorithm to lose money

Return Analysis

  • Absolute profit numbers are not sufficient to generate good returns, high profits could reduce returns if its capital utilization is high
  • Analysis of profit numbers with respect to capital utilization is done to grade each algorithm
  • Inferior algorithms are dropped to maintain the quality ROCE which captures the real returns instead of looking at profit numbers in an isolation

Variation Analysis

  • Not only returns but also its variation over time is analyzed using backtesting
  • Substandard algorithms are filtered out, for eg. algo losing over 25% a year or having consecutive years of negative returns etc
  • Variations need to be analyzed both at algorithm and portfolio level to reduce volatility

Portfolio Creation

  • The portfolio is as important as the algorithm itself
  • Correlation of the constituents, drawdown and capital utilization patterns are taken into consideration while building the portfolio
  • Portfolio building tools build portfolios best suited to the capital invested and investors’ requirements

Trade Simulation

  • A back-tested portfolio comes with the caveat “Historical returns may not be repeated in the future”
  • Trade simulation of portfolios is done in real-time to give you a feel of the performance in live market conditions
  • Real trading decisions are benefited from the simulation as it can dynamically identify the best performing algo when there are multiple simultaneous calls

Multiple Trading Strategies

  • Every call can be traded with different strategies
  • The strategies are designed based on the liquidity, volatility and risk appetite
  • Trades can vary from calls depending on investors’ style and preference
  • Several trading strategies are already built and can be customised to meet investors’ expectations
